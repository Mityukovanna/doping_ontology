# Example questions that are in-scope of the doping ontology

1. What items are prohibited only in-competition?
2. What items are prohibited in all times?
3. What non-specified substances are prohibited in all times?
4. In what sports beta-blockers are banned?
5. What health conditions allow use of the banned substance or method?
6. What WADA category/class does a substance belong to?
8. Which substances have permitted threshols rather than a zero-tolerance rule?
9. What physiological system is affected by the use of doping

# Example questions that are out of scope

1. What athletes were caught using doping?
2. What sanctions can athletes face if the doping-probes will be positive?
3. What methods are used to discover specific type of doping?
4. What is a chemical formula for a given type of banned substance?
5. When the item was banned?
